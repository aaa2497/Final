# Final Project "Can NYC Breathe?"

## Short Abstract
three sentence summary of the analysis you plan to do.
I plan to explore the relationship between particulate matter, or particle pollution made of an aggregate of chemicals, and pediatric asthma hospitlizations in NYC between 2009-2023.  The project will use Panda's, Matplotlib, X-array, and Numpy packages to graph and analyze the data. Essentially, the goal of the research is to explore if there are any spatial or temporal relationships between these factors, and investigate where gaps may emerge to provide local governance with more targeted efforts to improve air quality in vulnerable areas in the city. The study would have wider implications for enviromental and human health and security and provide context on historical redlining and environmental injustices to raise awareness about the right to clean air.

## Research Question:
### **Which boroughs in NYC experience higher air pollution (PM2.5), and does this relate to higher pediatric asthma rates over time? (2009-2023)**

## Hypothesis: 
### More industralized Boroughs will have higher air pollution rates leading to higher pediatric asthma hospitlizations**
